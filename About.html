Indian culture is a rich and diverse heritage of social norms and technologies that originated in or are associated with India. Various factors, such as arts and language, philosophy and religion, and food and drinks influence Indian culture. Some of the ancient and influential languages, religions, and cuisines of India have become globally important today.

Indian culture is a vibrant blend of traditions, rituals, and philosophies that have been shaped by its long history and diverse geography. It’s characterized by a rich tapestry of music, dance, art, and cuisine, each with regional variations.Family values are deeply rooted, with respect for elders and community at the core. Traditional attire varies, from sarees to turbans, symbolizing the country’s colorful diversity. Indian culture, thus, is a mosaic of ancient customs and modern influences, creating a unique and dynamic identity.
